About

Dotty Homes builds and remodels homes across Los Angeles and Orange County — for our neighbors, mostly.

We started Dotty the way most good shops start — one kitchen. The client told a neighbor, the neighbor told another, and somewhere along the way we became a construction company. Four years in, we still take on only what we can do well: a handful of projects at a time, with the same people you meet at the consultation on site every day until the walk‑through.

We like good neighbors, honest budgets, and the kind of detail nobody photographs but everyone notices. We answer our own phones, show up when we say we will, and design every floorplan ourselves.
